About

I’m a self-taught UI designer and I’ve been designing websites, apps, and emails for companies as a freelancer. While it genuinely brings me joy doing it, I always wanted to somehow advance my career within the same industry. In the course of acquiring and applying my visual design skills, it recently became evident that I was eager to build what I design.

HTML Email development was precisely what I was looking for. I was intrigued with the idea that I can not only deliver what I built but also indirectly help companies and organizations to achieve their goals. With my visual design skills and coding skills, I assure you that I am capable of delivering aesthetic and pixel-perfect emails for my clients.

As a freelancer, I’ve been working solo for the most part: however, I’m a punctual and hardworking developer who also can be a good team player. My ultimate goal as an HTML Email developer is to deliver effective emails with usability and accessibility in mind.
